Packages for Fedora 15 are emerging tomorrow. There's Branch Freeze event showing the "face" of the next Fedora version.

At the Branch Freeze event, all packages are branched from master in source control. This is to allow the branched tree to stabilize and enter a bug-fix and polish phase of development. Builds from the branch that are deemed stable will be published into their own repo on the mirrors beside rawhide.

There was higher package git activity this weekend. Fedora 15 will likely feature Rails 3 for example. And many more! I am coming with a longer list next week. Stay tuned!
